About KrishiConnect

Objectives of KrishiConnect

Design an intuitive and visually appealing interface tailored to farmers’ needs, enhancing usability and engagement.

Provide farmers with AI-based insights to determine the best crops to grow based on weather conditions.

Use real-time weather data to help farmers plan and manage their agricultural activities effectively.

Facilitate direct communication between farmers and wholesalers, reducing the dependency on middlemen.

Enable users to access the platform in multiple languages, making it inclusive for farmers across various regions.
